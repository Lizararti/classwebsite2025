Our History

TipToe Soles was established in 2008 as a small family-owned shoe store on the Central Coast. Over the years, we expanded our range and partnered with trusted comfort-footwear brands, becoming a local favourite for supportive, stylish shoes. Today, more than 15 years later, TipToe Soles continues to focus on quality, comfort, and personalised service.

Our Vision

Our vision at TipToe Soles is to be Australia’s most trusted destination for comfortable, stylish footwear. We aim to empower every customer to walk with confidence by offering shoes that combine support, durability, and modern design. We are committed to continuous improvement, embracing new technologies and sustainable practices to deliver long-lasting comfort for every step.

Our Team

The TipToe Soles team is made up of friendly, knowledgeable staff who are passionate about helping customers find the perfect fit. Our team members are trained in comfort footwear, customer service, and personalised fittings, ensuring every visitor receives supportive guidance and a welcoming experience. We work together to uphold the values that have shaped TipToe Soles since 2008 — honesty, care, and a commitment to quality.
